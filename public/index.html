What is IDEATHON 2021?

IDEATHON is a uniquely designed event that provides absolute beginners the perspective and the ability to start thinking entrepreneurially. Through this event participants can showcase their ideas in front of panelists and win prizes along with incubation support (for Technology Building, Idea Validation, Funding, Customer Connect, Commercialization, Scalability etc.)

ABOUT

Who can participate?

Any undergraduate student with innovative idea of product/ service with technology involved in it (hardware/ software/ both).

Benefits:

Support for Technology Development, Idea Validation, Funding, Customer Connect, Commercialization, Scalability, IPR, and for registration of firm on various portals.

Focused sectors:

Service or Manufacturing in: Agriculture Technology, Healthcare, Industrial IoT (IIoT), Smart Product, Sanitation and Waste Management, Education, Food, Finance, Robotics, Automation in Animal Husbandry

Winners will be selected based upon following Parameters


Need of Product/ Services to Society
Commercial Viability of Idea
Innovative Component
Competitor Analysis
Unique Selling Points (features)
Startup Journey Map (timeline of Ideation to Commercialization)
Team Strength
Business Model
Product Services
